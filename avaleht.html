My journey into UX/UI design began at the end of 2024 when I discovered this kind of pursuit through my own persona. I remember searching up something like best career choices for artists and got an answer, which was simply a list made of jobs. I knew for myself I did not want to create art through traditional mediums, whether it would be a painter, a sculptor - technology striked my interest more. Since I had already done some of my works digitally, like illustrations and photo edits, I knew I had to choose something I was already familiar with. UX/UI designer struck like a lightning bolt through my darkened vision of what I could become, as I found out how many similarities it has with my passions.
